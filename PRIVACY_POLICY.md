# Privacy Policy for Dipandu Exam Assistant

**Last Updated: January 17, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Dipandu Exam Assistant ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your use of our Chrome Extension.

## 1. Information Collection

**Personal Information**: We do not collect, store, or share any personal identification information (PII) from our users.

**Data Storage**:
-   **API Keys**: Your OpenRouter API Key is stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.sync`. It is never transmitted to our servers.
-   **History**: Analysis history (screenshots and text responses) is stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`.

## 2. Information Usage

The data captured by the extension (screenshots and text prompts) is transmitted directly from your browser to the OpenRouter API for the sole purpose of generating an AI response. We do not have access to this data, and it does not pass through any intermediate servers owned by us.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use **OpenRouter** as our AI provider. Please refer to OpenRouter's privacy policy to understand how they handle the data sent to their API.

## 4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at [github.com/theyudhiztira](https://github.com/theyudhiztira).
